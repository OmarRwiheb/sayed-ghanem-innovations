SG INNOVATIONS

Sayed Ghanem Innovations is known as a full-service company with its experience spanning architecture, luxury interior design, construction, engineering supervision, integrated finishes, special customer services, and cost management efficiency.

It was established by Engineer Sayed Mohamed Wael since 2017, and it’s located in 6th of October City (Egypt).

We are proud of all the projects that have been delivered with excellence, ingenuity and perfection under Sayed Ghanem Innovations’ belt. We pride ourselves on our ability to create innovative solutions that create a symphony of design that exceeds the most expectations of the client.
